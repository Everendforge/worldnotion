# Resonance Debt

Resonance Debt is the price paid when a traveler forces an [[Old Roads]] route to remember them.

The debt can be paid with memory, an oath, or a carried flame from the [[Cinder Lantern]].

The [[Ashen Choir]] calls it justice. [[Mara Voss]] calls it blackmail.
