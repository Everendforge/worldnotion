# First Road

The First Road is the original decision-route beneath [[Ash Gate]].

Unlike the [[Old Roads]], it does not connect places. It connects a person to the version of themselves that made a different promise.

The [[Iron Meridian]] can locate it only after the player resolves who owns the [[Glass Key]].
