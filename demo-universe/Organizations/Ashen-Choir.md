# Ashen Choir

The Ashen Choir guards the thresholds below [[Ash Gate]] and teaches that every road has a voice.

They cooperate with the [[Glass Cartographers]] in public, but privately they steal surveys that mention the [[Old Roads]].

Their oldest rite requires the [[Glass Key]] and a traveler willing to accept a [[Resonance Debt]].
