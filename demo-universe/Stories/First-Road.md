# First Road

Mara Voss follows the [[Iron Meridian]] from the lower archive of [[Ash Gate]] into the [[Ember Cistern]].

The path splits around three questions: who holds the [[Glass Key]], whether the [[Ashen Choir]] can be trusted, and what kind of [[Resonance Debt]] Mara is willing to carry.
