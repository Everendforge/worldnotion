# Road Under Ash

The [[First Road]] opens beneath the [[Ember Cistern]] and asks [[Mara Voss]] which promise she wants to preserve.

The ending depends on whether the player carries the [[Glass Key]], the [[Cinder Lantern]], or an unpaid [[Resonance Debt]].
