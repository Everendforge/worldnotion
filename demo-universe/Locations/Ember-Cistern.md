# Ember Cistern

The Ember Cistern sits below [[Ash Gate]], where hot springs keep the old tunnels breathing.

Its sluice gates hide a marker for the [[First Road]], but the [[Ashen Choir]] marks every trespass with ash-thread.

The [[Cinder Lantern]] burns blue when the cistern is lying.
