# Sera Nym

Sera Nym is a silent archivist embedded inside the [[Glass Cartographers]] on behalf of the [[Ashen Choir]].

She believes the [[First Road]] should stay buried because every opened route demands a [[Resonance Debt]] from the traveler who names it.

## Branch Use

Sera is the pressure point for routes that trade knowledge for safety.
