# Kellan Or

Kellan Or is a cistern scout who sells route rumors to anyone willing to cross the [[Old Roads]].

He once carried the [[Glass Key]] for the [[Ashen Choir]], but he claims the key changed hands before [[Mara Voss]] arrived at [[Ash Gate]].

## Playable Pressure

- Kellan can become an ally if Mara shares the truth of the [[Iron Meridian]].
- He withholds cistern access unless the player has the [[Cinder Lantern]] or a favor from the [[Glass Cartographers]].
