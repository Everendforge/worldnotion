# Glass Key

The Glass Key is a brittle charm that opens cartographic locks in the lower archive.

It belonged to [[Kellan Or]] before [[Sera Nym]] claimed it for the [[Ashen Choir]].

It can tune the [[Iron Meridian]] toward the [[First Road]] or shatter to erase one [[Resonance Debt]].
