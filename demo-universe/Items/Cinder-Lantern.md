# Cinder Lantern

The Cinder Lantern is a miner's lamp modified by the [[Glass Cartographers]] to reveal false passages.

Its blue flame exposes ash-thread traps inside the [[Ember Cistern]].

When carried onto the [[First Road]], it can absorb one [[Resonance Debt]] before going dark.
